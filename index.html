Google Adwords, SEO & PPC Management
Over 80% of search results now contain AdWords ad placements and those placements can cover as much as 85% of the space above the fold on the results page. We set up and manage ad word campaigns. AdWords allows you to get up and running very quickly. As soon as your account is created and your ads are approved, they’ll start displaying in search results and your site will start receiving traffic.

Pay-per-click campaigns can also provide you with vital data that can be used to plan your organic SEO campaigns. With AdWords, you’ll quickly know which keywords are able to drive the most traffic and which drive the most conversions. This will let you know what you should strive to rank for in the organic results.

SEO is measurable – every campaign can be measured, calculated and corrected. Customers use social media, it was recently found 83% of UK online shoppers use search engines to research a purchase. It is important to invest heavily, as one position above your competitions in search results will instantly result in more customers coming to your website. The more your site is optimised, the better rankings in search engines, the more traffic you will generate and the more lead/sales you will get. There is a high drop off in click through rate between the first and second positions on the first page, so WE make sure you outrank your competitors.
